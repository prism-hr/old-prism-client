We’re looking for Electronics and Computer Science graduates keen to start making real products for our clients. You’ll need to be the sort of person who is willing to turn their hand to a lot of different tasks, and enjoys constantly learning new things.

Background:

Biocatalysts is a customised enzyme discovery, development and manufacturing company based in the UK, with a subsidiary in Illinois, USA. We work with our customers, many of which are the top blue chip food companies, to provide unique enzyme solutions that will help them solve process problems, valorise waste, facilitate a biochemical reaction or deliver competitive advantage through using a unique product.

Due to our on-going commitment to expanding our technical capabilities within our enzyme production plant, we have an opportunity for a Process Development Specialist who will be part of our highly motivated Process Development Team.

Purpose of the role:

To develop and deliver commercially successful fermentation and downstream processing for enzyme products. To deliver enzyme materials on time to meet customer orders and project objectives.

Profile:

You will have a degree in Biochemical Engineering or equivalent. Having practical experience in fermentation, downstream processing, technical transfer and scale up or working in a technical environment would be a distinct advantage. 

If you are someone who is practical, ‘hands on’ with a flexible, ‘can-do’ attitude, analytical, with great
attention to detail and willingness to change working hours and work extra to accommodate fermentation processes, please contact us today.

Hours:
09:00 – 17:00 during training period and then you will be required to work 4 x 11 hr shifts 07:00 - 18:00

Substantial benefits also include Life Insurance, Childcare Vouchers, free parking and others.
